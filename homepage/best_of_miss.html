LLT IS A 2016
'BEST OF MISSISSIPPI'
We're tickled pink!

Mississippi Magazine's annual "BEST OF MISSISSIPPI" list for 2016 announced LLT was selected as one of the TOP FIVE performance venues in the state! The list includes the MSU Riley Center in Meridian, the Bologna Performing Arts Center in Cleveland, Duling Hall and New Stage Theatre in Jackson and Laurel Little Theatre in Laurel.

Thanks to all our volunteer workers, as well as our season members and patrons, for continually supporting LLT and our beautiful and historic 1927 Arabian Theatre!
